Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quik dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, diced vegetables, cooked meas, herbs

Instruction

  1. Beat the eggs: In a bowl,beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper untill they are well mixed. You can add a tablespoon of water or milk for a fluffier texture.
  2. Heat the pan: Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.
  3. Cook the omelette: Once the butter is melted and buddling, pour in the the eggs. Tilt the pan to ensure the eggs evenly coat the surface
  4. Adding fillings (optional): When the eggs begin to set at the edges but are still slighly running in te middle, sprinkle your chosen fillings over the one half of the omelette.
  5. Fold and serve: As the omelette continues to cook, carefully lift one edge and fold it over the filling. Let it cook for another minute, then slide it onto a plate.
  6. Enjoy: Serve hot, with additional salt and pepper if needed.
